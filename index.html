The American Fender Stratocaster

Stratocasters are one of the most popular, and most copied, electric guitar shapes in history. The Fender Stratocaster was designed in 1954 as a solid body double cutaway guitar with extended top horn shape and three single coil pickups. It was originally produced with a solid ash body-in 1956 Fender began issuing with alder bodies. Vintage Fender Stratocasters are highly coveted by collectors and players who prefer the timbre of older models.
